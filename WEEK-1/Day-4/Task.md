# Task 1 – Automation Thinking

## 1. Auto Email After Registration
When a student completes registration, Salesforce automatically sends a confirmation email with registration details and instructions.

### Why Automation Helps
- Reduces manual communication
- Improves response speed
- Enhances student experience

---

## 2. Auto Update Remaining Seats
Whenever a student enrolls in a course, the available seat count is automatically reduced.

### Why Automation Helps
- Prevents overbooking
- Maintains accurate course capacity
- Saves administrative effort

---

## 3. Notify Faculty When Course is Full
When a course reaches maximum capacity, an automatic notification is sent to faculty and administration.

### Why Automation Helps
- Improves course management
- Helps departments plan additional sections
- Avoids confusion

---

## 4. Generate Student ID Automatically
When a new student record is created, the system automatically generates a unique student ID.

### Why Automation Helps
- Eliminates manual ID generation
- Ensures uniqueness
- Improves consistency

---

## 5. Send Fee Deadline Reminder
Before fee due dates, students automatically receive reminder emails or notifications.

### Why Automation Helps
- Improves fee collection
- Reduces missed deadlines
- Saves staff time

# Task 3 – Manual vs Automated Process

## Process Chosen
Student Fee Payment Reminder

---

## Manual Process

1. Staff manually checks pending fee records  
2. Staff manually prepares reminder messages  
3. Reminder emails are sent one by one  
4. Staff tracks payments manually  
5. Follow-ups are done manually  

### Problems in Manual Process
- Human errors may occur
- Delayed communication
- Increased workload for staff
- Difficult to track reminders
- Time-consuming process

---

## Automated Process Using Salesforce

Using Salesforce Flow:

1. The system automatically checks fee due dates  
2. Reminder emails are sent automatically  
3. Payment status is updated automatically  
4. Follow-up reminders are scheduled  
5. All reminders are tracked in the system  

### Benefits of Automation
- Faster communication
- Better accuracy
- Reduced manual effort
- Improved productivity
- Better tracking and monitoring

---

# Conclusion

Automation simplifies repetitive tasks and helps organizations manage student fee reminders efficiently. Using Salesforce Flow improves speed, consistency, and accuracy compared to manual processes.
