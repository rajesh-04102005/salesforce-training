## Explain how CRM concepts fit into Salesforce Platform

CRM concepts in Salesforce are implemented using standard objects inside Apps.

| CRM Concept | Salesforce Representation |
|---|---|
| Account | Standard Object |
| Contact | Standard Object |
| Opportunity | Standard Object |
| Lead | Standard Object |

### Explanation

- Account represents a company or organization.
- Contact represents a person associated with an account.
- Opportunity represents a possible sales deal.
- Lead represents a potential customer before qualification.

These objects are organized inside Salesforce Apps such as the Sales App.

### CRM Flow

```text
Lead → Account → Contact → Opportunity
