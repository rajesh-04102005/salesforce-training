# Reflective Questions – Day 6

## 1. Why do systems need triggers?

Triggers help systems react automatically to events and reduce manual work.

---

## 2. Difference between polling and event-driven systems?

Polling continuously checks for updates while event-driven systems react only when events happen.

---

## 3. Why are database queries important?

Queries help retrieve important data quickly and efficiently.

---

## 4. When should Flows be preferred over Triggers?

Flows are preferred for simple automation without coding.

---

## 5. What problems happen if automation becomes too complex?

Complex automation becomes difficult to debug and maintain.

---

## 6. Why should developers think carefully before automating actions?

Bad automation can create performance and business issues.
