# Revision Questions & Answers

## 1. Why are tests important in enterprise systems?
Tests help ensure reliability, prevent bugs, and improve software quality.

## 2. What problems happen without testing?
Applications may contain bugs, incorrect logic, crashes, and unreliable workflows.

## 3. Why is asynchronous processing useful?
It allows long-running operations to execute in the background without slowing users.

## 4. Difference between synchronous and asynchronous processing?
Synchronous processing runs immediately while asynchronous processing runs in the background.

## 5. Why do developers use version control?
Version control helps track changes, collaborate safely, and manage code history.

## 6. Why is GitHub important?
GitHub helps teams collaborate, store code, and manage projects efficiently.

## 7. Why is DX useful for teams?
Salesforce DX supports modern development workflow and team collaboration.

## 8. How do Flows, Triggers and Validation Rules work together?
Validation Rules verify data, Flows automate actions, and Triggers handle backend logic.

## 9. Why should business logic be tested carefully?
Incorrect business logic can affect users, data accuracy, and system reliability.

## 10. Why is developer workflow important in large teams?
Structured workflow improves collaboration, reduces errors, and increases productivity.
